Webhook

Overview

Webhooks are a way of receiving event data for your calls and associated resources by your own servers.

Notification

The VoIPBIN sends the webhook event for event notification.

Types of webhooks

Each resources in the voipbin generates its own webhooks to provide the progress.

Webhook
